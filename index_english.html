About Who

Graffiti and Street Art

Who

Matheus Boufleur Cunha

I am Matheus Boufleur Cunha and I sign my works as Who.

I have always enjoyed drawing and I have discovered my passion for art through stencil paintings.

From skateboarding and hip hop culture, I have started using free hand spray paintings, so I have entered into the graffiti's world.

I produce paintings and canvases with elements of street art. I also do commercial spray work and record my style on city walls.

To know me better there is @whograff on instagram and other social networks: twitter, facebook and youtube.

For me, each work has a meaning, but the interesting thing about my work is that each one gives the meaning that makes sense.

Understand what I do ...

Street Art

Interventions through the city. Visual art that makes people create their own meanings and reflections.

Paintings and Canvases

Intimate creation. Framed designs made with various materials for different environments.

Commercial art

Exclusive productions for commercial environments. Spray and graffiti arts, focused on the company's visual identity and product/service advertising.

Customizations

Objects and residential environments customization. I have worked with various objects and environments, such as spray cans, clothing, bedrooms, pools, living rooms, doors and furniture.
